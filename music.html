Music

I love listening to music, sometimes a bit too much. I mostly listen to Rock, Hip hop Rap, and Electronic Music and the related subgenres. However, it's not really a hard limit, as long as I like what I'm listening to, I'm gonna listen to it. I'm one of those people who listen to the same music over and over again, instead of discovering new music.

Enough about my listening habbits. A few years back I started getting into gamedev, and obviously I was watching Brackeys (YAYY he's back). There, he introduced Bosca Ceoil (damn, it has a new open source remake now), for making music for the games we make. And man, that sent me down quite the rabbit hole. I eventually got bored of gamedev, but I really enjoy Music Production, I've been getting into Sound Synthesis recently. I'm no Giovanni Giorgio, but it seems really fun. I don't own any hardware Synths (YET), but my software synthesizer of choice is Vital, an amazing open source synth, made by the amazing Matt Tytel. I use Ardour or LMMS as my DAW(s) of choice. Haven't made something that I'm brave enough to show here yet, maybe someday I'll add a soundcloud link here.
